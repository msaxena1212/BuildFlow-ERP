# Construction Management System Demo Overview

BuildFlow ERP is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade solution designed to revolutionize construction project lifecycles. This document outlines the core modules, structural management, inventory controls, and site operations that define the platform.

---

## 🚀 System Overview
- **Comprehensive ERP Solution:** A unified digital nervous system for construction projects of any scale.
- **Core Modules:**
  - **Task & Project Management:** High-fidelity tracking from soil testing to final handover.
  - **Site Management:** Real-time tracking of site activities, safety audits, and progress.
  - **Warehouse & Inventory:** End-to-end control over material lifecycles and stock levels.
  - **Stock Tracking:** Automated monitoring of in/out transactions with SKU-level precision.
  - **Communication Suite:** Native WhatsApp and email integration for automated alerts and stakeholder updates.
  - **Governance:** Multi-level approval workflows for financial and operational compliance.

---

## 🏗️ Project Structure & Management
- **Diverse Project Support:**
  - Commercial Renovation
  - Residential New Building
  - Industrial Facility
  - Infrastructure Projects (Highways, Bridges, Utilities)
- **Geospatial Intelligence:** Google Maps API integration for automatic location fetching, weather alerts, and logistics planning.
- **Milestone-Based Breakdown:**
  - **Structural Phases:** Foundation, Structural Framing, Roofing, MEP, and Interior Finishing.
  - **Hierarchy:** Support for sub-milestones and complex task hierarchies.
  - **Visual Execution:** Production Kanban board with drag-and-drop status updates (To Do ➔ In Progress ➔ Review ➔ Completed).
- **Advanced Scheduling:**
  - Sequential and parallel workflow options.
  - **CPM Engine:** Support for Finish-to-Start (FS) and Finish-to-Finish (FF) task dependencies.

---

## 📦 Material Management & Inventory
- **Real-Time Tracking:** Automated inventory monitoring with configurable **Min/Max limits** and critical buffer alerts.
- **Consumption Analytics:** Task-wise material consumption tracking to identify wastage or theft.
- **Hierarchical Requisition Workflow:**
  - **Site Manager:** Authority for approvals up to **100kg**.
  - **Warehouse Manager:** Authority for approvals up to **500kg**.
  - **Management:** Required for approvals exceeding **1000kg**.
- **Intelligence Alerts:** Automatic quantity alerts triggered when actual consumption exceeds project projections.
- **Vendor Ecosystem:** Full vendor management with integrated RFQ (Request for Quotation) and bidding logic.
- **Allocation Logic:** Sophisticated material allocation and deallocation workflows to optimize site resources.

---

## 👷 Site Operations & Documentation
- **Field Intelligence:** Real-time photo uploads with mandatory **geotagging and timestamps** for immutable site records.
- **Progress Tracking:** Granular progress percentage tracking with daily site updates and automatic schedule synchronization.
- **Labor Portal:** Dedicated portal for site workers and foremen to manage daily tasks and safety protocols.
- **Document Vault:** Centralized management for blueprints, legal documents, floor plans, and architectural drawings.
- **Automated Reporting:** Daily and weekly progress reports auto-generated in **PDF and Excel** formats for stakeholder transparency.
